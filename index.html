Davide Mazzaccara

I am a PhD Student in Computational Linguistics/Natural Language Processing at CIMeC, University of Trento, supervised by Raffaella Bernardi. In the LaVI group, I am working on reasoning with language models, questions and language games. I previously studied Linguistics at the University of Pavia, as a student of the Collegio Borromeo. My research interests include instruction following and pragmatics, computational semantics and interpretability.
